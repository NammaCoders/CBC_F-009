Did you know

  • 1 in 10 adults worldwide has diabetes, and nearly half of them are unaware of it.
  • Type 2 diabetes is preventable in many cases with healthy eating and regular exercise.
  • Early symptoms of diabetes include frequent urination, extreme thirst, and unexplained weight loss.
  • Prediabetes often has no symptoms, but lifestyle changes at this stage can stop diabetes from developing.
